Everything About Manufacturing

_ _ _

Manufacturing software, also known as MES or MOM software, is a crucial tool for modern industrial operations. It facilitates efficient and organized production processes, from sourcing raw materials to delivering the finished products. Key functions include production scheduling, quality control, inventory management, and machine monitoring, all of which help companies enhance productivity, reduce costs, and maintain consistent product quality. With the automation and digitization capabilities it offers, manufacturing software is essential for businesses aiming to stay competitive in today's rapidly evolving industrial landscape.

Features Of Admin

2. Set up discounts for the products
3. View and manage orders

Features of the Seller Panel

3. Place orders and check order history and status.
